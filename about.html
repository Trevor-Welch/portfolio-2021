A Bit About Me

I’m a 27-year-old environmentally concerned digital creative currently living in the city of Durham, North Carolina.

Right now, I’m social distancing by staying indoors and improving my coding skills by finishing off my web design degree at Wake Tech. (Only one semester left!)

With my free time, I’m using my digital skills to help non-profits and small business owners who need a bit of volunteer work to help them float through the COVID-19 pandemic.

After I graduate, I aim to work with other individuals who are passionate about using modern technology and great design to deliver incredible experiences and products to people in a way that is environmentally conscious, socially inclusive, and accessible to all. Until then, I will be having fun with code, exploring my creativity, and trying to find the best cup of coffee in town.

In a more distant future, I’d love to one day find myself not only creating websites and graphics, but also games and experiences in the realm of virtual reality. Video games have always been a passion of mine and after getting a VR headset, I realized immediately just how much potential the technology has. It’s not in my skillset yet, but it’s a long-term goal of mine.
